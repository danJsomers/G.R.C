About The PlayStation consoles

PlayStation 3

The PS3 was released on March 23 2007. It was competing against the Xbox 360. This was the first time that Sony introduced a network feature officially named PlayStation Network (PSN) where players could play online with friends and other people worldwide. The PlayStation 3 has sold 85 million units across the globe.

PlayStation 4

The PS4 was released on November 29 2013. Its main competitor this time was the Xbox One. The interesting thing about this one was that you were able to play games off console on the mobile devices by Sony by using “Remote Play”. Also there was a new ability to Live Stream using “Share Play” to friends or to a community if you were more well known. Unfortunately a paid membership was required to access to ability to play online with others when it was free for the PS3. More than 53 million consoles were sold worldwide.

PlayStation 4 PRO

The PRO version of the PS4 introduced the PlayStation Virtual Reality Headset (PSVR) into gameplay. Although it can be bought for the original PS4, the PRO version has it included in the price. It also holds more memory, allowing more games to be stored and played.
