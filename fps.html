DOOM

DOOM is a FPS that focuses on making the player incredibly over powered due to the weapons and the brutal personality of the unnamed protagonist that the community just refer to as “DOOMGuy”. It is often set in Hell as the player single handedly ripps through waves and waves of demons of all shapes and sizes; becoming feared by the occupants of hell. The game originally started as a fairly basic 3D First Person Shooter and later progressed to the latest installment in 2016 that was a call back to the very first original game with updated graphics, thrilling gameplay and hidden “Easter Eggs”(secrets) that relate to previous games in the franchise

Half Life

Half Life 2 is the FPS that practically every gamer knows about and was developed by Valve Corporation. The game is split into a few chapters and focuses around the main character- Gordon Freeman as he tries to defend against the multidimensional empire known as the “Combine”. The game is a direct sequel to the first game and gameplay wise it is very similar, only this one focuses more around the physics of the game engine, an example is when you pick up the “Gravity Gun” where you can pull objects towards you, hold them mid-air then repel them at great speed preferably at an enemy. It is particularly devastating if the object is explosive or sharp.

Call of Duty

Call of Duty (CoD) is the smash hit FPS game that is beginning to get old. It usually is set around war or terrorist attacks with a large arsenal of gadgets and weapons to be used. Although the developers have tried to innovate the franchise by including things like jetpacks and wall running, the gameplay doesn't stray too far from its core leaving it to become quite repetitive. The online multiplayer is also quite lackluster as professional and skilled players are put into matches with new or less skilled players which makes it difficult for new people to enjoy multiplayer and/or get better as the better players will keep killing the less knowledgeable. In addition to this the people who play longer get access to more powerful guns and gadgets making it extremely unfair for the new recruits.
