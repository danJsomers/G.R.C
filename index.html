PlayStation

The PlayStation consoles are created by Sony Interactive Entertainment and have been around from 1994 with the release of the PlayStation (the first console). Since then they have released 3 more consoles, an upgraded version of the most recent one and handheld devices known as the PS Vita. PlayStation have also created an exclusive Virtual Reality (VR) headset that can be bought separately or included with the upgraded model.

Xbox

The Xbox consoles were created by Microsoft and started a little late into the console war as the first Xbox was released in 2002 becoming a direct competitor to Sony’s PlayStation 2. Microsoft was well known for their computers giving them a boost when it came to sales for the first console but couldn't get close to the PS2 as Sony had already been well established after the PS1’s success. So far they have released 3 consoles with an extra upgraded version of their most recent one.

PC Gaming

The best computers made for gaming would come from manufacturers such as Alienware or Omen however as computers aren't all made for gaming (although some are) and anyone can get a game on any make it is better to focus on where to get the games, the software. These are online game sites where you can pay for a game digitally and download it onto your computer. Such sites include: Steam, uPlay and Origin.
