Portal 2

Portal 2 is a game made by Valve. It is a 3D FPS but you don’t hold a bullet firing gun. The device you hold creates two connected portals that can be placed on walls made of moon rocks. You play as the silent protagonist Chell as she gets ridiculed by the Artificial Intelligence known as GLaDOS whilst trying to escape the facility. The puzzles are intriguing as you have to think with portals and that you can walk between two places in space and time the portals will use momentum as well so if you fall from a great height into a portal on the ground below and out of a portal on an angled wall you’ll fly out at the speed you went in. Thanks to specially designed boots you can fall lethal distances without death.

N++

N++ is a 2D level based game that has a lot of hazards to avoid and utilises momentum. You play as a ninja like stick figure as you leap, wall jump, bounce, dodge, slide, collect things and die around the levels. Not only are there over 500 levels in the “campaign” but there is a level editor and a community levels page where you can play even more levels, it's endless.

Inside

Inside is a 2.5D side scrolling environmental puzzle platformer. You play as a young boy that must find his way through a world that seems to be controlling the minds of civilians. Obstacles constantly block your way and you must find a way around the problem, sometimes there is a time limit. There are a massive number of things that can kill your character so you have to be quick and efficient.
