Motorstorm

Motorstorm Apocalypse is a tremendously gripping racing game exclusive to the PS3, where the environment tumbles around you, changing the track for later laps! In the campaign and single player these moments can be zoomed into slowing time down and making the experience more epic. There are a load of different vehicles to choose from all having a unique play style; some are fast and need flat, dry ground to keep the momentum like the Supercar, others can power through uneven terrain and mud with ease like the Monster Truck. Multiplayer allows you to go against other players with their choice of vehicle and in online multiplayer you can bet on people that you expect to beat.

Trackmania Turbo

Trackmania Turbo is a real rush of adrenaline. Unlike other racers out there Trackmania Turbo plays more like the middle ground between a karting game and a realistic racer; at times players can experience humongous jumps, go at incredible speeds and go around loops whilst doing huge drifts like karting games, other times your braking into a corner and taking racing lines like a realistic racer or traversing dirt/sand like rally car racers. Users can build their own tracks and play others made by fellow users in online play or play games in split screen, hotseat or arcade mode if in the same room; you can even activate weapons and/or environmental hazards!

Forza

Forza is a realistic racer with exclusive for the Xbox cars that resemble real life cars such as the Mclaren P1 seen in the image above. The later installments of Forza Motorsport (4-5) have included commentary from the presenters of the famous BBC TV programme “Top Gear”: Jeremy Clarkson, Richard Hammond and James May.
