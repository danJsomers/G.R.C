Minecraft

Everyone and their grandmother has heard of Minecraft before, it's a game that is one of the best selling games of all time! If for some reason you don’t know, Minecraft is a open world survival and building game. In survival mode, you have a health and food bar that you have to maintain in order to not die. When you spawn into the randomly generated terrain you then need to collect resources to progress and stay alive. Although there is an official end to the game, there isn’t a campaign therefore you are free to do whatever it is that you want to do. You could reach the end or you could just build things- minigames, houses, castles, redstone contraptions, farms etc, the possibilities are endless.

Alien: Isolation

Want a good scare? Alien: Isolation is one of the best survival horror games out there. It starts by building the atmosphere and allowing you to get used to the controls, then it throws danger in your face! From panicking, trigger happy humans to corrupt androids to a savage terrifying Alien! In this game you have to be stealthy to avoid danger, eventually though, you will have to defend yourself by finding weapons or crafting them from other bits and pieces. Add a motion tracker that beeps faster the closer a hostile entity is to your position and you’ve got yourself a horror game you’ll be afraid to pick up the controller to play.

Subnautica

Subnautica is an underwater nightmare, you are a crew member of a spaceship that gets stranded alone on a planet that is 99.9% water after the ship crashes into it. The water is literally swimming with life but not all the creatures are docile; in fact most want to kill you. In order to survive you need to collect resources, build an underwater base and build water transport to help you traverse the oceans.
